Extra Credit

For your final project, you must analyze either a piece of visual media or the five types of vegetarians. I’m excited to read what you put together for your classmates’ assignment sheets, but there’s one problem: I don’t get to read what you would’ve written if we had picked your assignment.

So: for 5 potential points of extra credit, please write 500 words responding to the assignment sheet that you wrote. Your 500 words might be the introduction, an outline/plan, or some combination of both.

This is extra credit and is consequently relatively informal. However, please keep in mind that I will be evaluating it based on the criteria you specified.

If your name is Ashley or Elliot, you may submit your response to Ashley or Elliot’s assignment sheet to complete this extra credit assignment.

This extra credit opportunity should be submitted to me via email by the end of finals week.
