Final Project

There are two options for completing the final paper for this class. You can:

  • Analyze a piece of visual media or
  • Evaluate the five types of vegetarian.

Whichever option you select, the first draft will be due on Eli Review on May 2. A peer review session will follow on May 4.

Note: Your Eli Review grade is a completion average; this means I grade it by adding the number of Eli Review assignments you complete (on time) and dividing that number by the total number of Eli Review assignments which exist. The first draft of the final paper and the peer review which accompanies it are your last opportunity to earn Eli Review points. Please, for the love of all that is holy, submit something for these two assignments.

The final draft of the final project should be emailed to me by May 9. It should be a Word document with a .docx extension, and should be 1,250 words in length.
