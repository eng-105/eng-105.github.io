Posting Your Portfolio to UBLearns

Before graduating from UB, you will take the required Capstone course. In this class, you’ll put together a portfolio of work you completed in your classes and take some time to reflect on your learning.

To prepare for this, please upload one of your essays from this semester to the UBLearns portfolio tool, using the following instructions. Upload your favorite one. You can upload additional work if you’d like, as well.

Please note: every semester, there are numerous complaints from students who failed to do this simple task. If you fail to save any of your work, the Capstone course is very difficult to complete!

Also, it’s fun. Think how great it will be when you read a paper you wrote this semester and can see how much you’ve improved.

Step 1

Go to UBLearns; click on the “Tools” link.

Step 2

Click “Portfolios”

Step 3

Click “My Artifacts.”

Step 4

Click “Add Personal Artifact.”

Step 5

  • Fill out the “Title” field. You might want to be detailed about this. “ENG105 Research Essay” or “Spring 2022 Day in the Life project” are good. You might forget what the assignment was about or what class it was for!
  • Click “Browse Local Files” to add your assignment. You can upload a PDF or Word document of your paper, but personally, I would upload a Word file. If in two years you’re better at catching typos, you might want to fix them.
  • Click “Submit,” obviously.

And you’re done!
