Readings must be completed before class the day they are assigned. It is expected that you will have read them, have taken notes on them, and will bring questions or comments to class on them.

In general, expect to submit a piece of writing to Eli Review every Friday, and to give feedback to your peers every Monday. Other assignments may be due throughout the week, so consult this schedule often. All assignments are always due by 11:59pm.
