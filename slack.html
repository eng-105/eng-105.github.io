Distance Learning

Our subsitute for in-class participation will occur on Slack. You will participate by:

  • Responding to any questions I ask.
  • Posting three comments or questions to the #general channel per week. This is exactly like raising your hand: you can ask about upcoming assignments, post your thoughts on one of the readings for the week, or whatever. Unlike speech, however, you can Slack links, files, memes, whatever.
  • Responding to two of your peers' posts per week.

My DMs on Slack will always be open, as they say. Email will be fine too. There will be another channel for announcements, reminders, important dates. I am happy to video conference with you, on any platform you prefer, at any time. Just let me know.
