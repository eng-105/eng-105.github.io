Eli Review

Eli Review is a core component of this course.

Participation

Participation in this course includes all forms of verbal participation in class, including asking questions or offering suggestions/feedback to your peers and to me.

More generally, in-class participation is a way of signalling – to me and especially to your peers – that you are thoughtfully engaging in our mutual production of ideas.

Participation also includes engagement in in-class activities and writing assignments, and performances on pop quizzes which I will occasionally administer with regard to the day's readings.

Presentation

The presentation is an opportunity for you to hone your oral rhetorical skills.
