Evaluating Vegetarianism

For the final assignment for class, you will have to research vegetarians and the different kinds of vegetarians. You will write about the pros and cons of each vegetarian. You will find each vegetarian and list the many pros and cons of each one. Learning the pros and cons of each one allows you to understand the history and the reason why people follow these kinds of diets. The benefits of being a vegetarian can also influence you as a person to try the diet.

The assignment will be evaluated by the amount of information with at least 5 pros and cons of each kind of vegetarianism. It will also be evaluated by how well the information is given and how they connect to each other. You also need to give the information clearly, as well as have at least 5 citations.

You will learn the skills of how to give information properly as well as learn the differences and the health benefits of each vegetarian.

Requirements

Your paper should:

  • Identify five varieties of vegetarian;
  • Identify five pros and cons of each;
  • Identify reasons individuals decide to become vegetarian;
  • Contain historical information about the development of vegetarianism;
  • Cite five sources in MLA format.

Fine Print

This assignment is worth 10% of your final grade. The first draft is due May 2 on Eli Review. There will then be a peer review session due on May 4. The final draft should be submitted via email by May 9. The final draft should be 1250 words.
